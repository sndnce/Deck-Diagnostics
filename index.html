About Deck Diagnostics

Deck Diagnostics, or “Deck Doctor”, is a division of Home Dev Co dedicated to helping landlords meet the SB-721 mandatory inspection deadline of January 1, 2026. This law requires inspections to determine whether a structure poses an immediate safety threat.

If an issue is found, action must be taken without delay.
