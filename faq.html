WHAT IS THE TYPICAL TIMELINE OF SB-721 REPAIRS?

Average time: about 9 months (≈ 285 days).

  • After your inspection, you’ll receive the stamped report in about 45 days.
  • If permits are required, they take about 120 days from the report date.
  • Repairs are typically completed ~120 days after the permit is approved.
WHAT IS THE AVERAGE COST OF SB-721 REPAIRS?

Waiting increases cost. Labor and materials rise as the deadline approaches.

  • As of August 2023, the average repair cost was $60,000.
  • By January 1, 2025, costs are expected to be about 50% higher (~$120,000).

Tip: Acting now can save you significant money.

WHAT FALLS UNDER SB-721 REGULATIONS?

Any exterior elevated elements made of wood-framed or steel-framed construction, including:

  • Cantilevered decks & guard rails
  • Balconies & guard rails
  • Exterior walkways
  • Entrance & exit balconies
  • Exterior stairs, landings, and guard rails
WHY SHOULD I GET MY PROPERTY CHECKED BEFORE THE DEADLINE?
  • Repair costs are rising quickly due to high demand.
  • Owners/Landlords are responsible for all repair costs.
  • Delays can lead to daily fines and higher insurance premiums.
  • If you sell after the deadline without repairs, you cover the full expense as the seller.
