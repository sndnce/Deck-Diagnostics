Frequently Asked Questions

What is SB-721?

SB-721 is a California state law that requires periodic inspections of certain exterior elements in multi-family residential buildings, such as balconies, decks, and walkways.

Who does this law apply to?

It applies to buildings with 3 or more multi-family dwelling units that have exterior elevated elements more than 6 feet above ground level.

How often do inspections need to happen?

The first inspection must be completed by January 1, 2026, and then once every six years thereafter.

What happens if I don’t comply?

Failure to comply with the timeline may result in daily fines of $100–$500 per affected unit.

How long does the inspection take?

Most inspections can be completed within a single site visit. Reports are delivered within a few business days.
